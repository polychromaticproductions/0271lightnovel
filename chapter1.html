CHAPTER 1

A dull-clothed ragged girl walks across the rooftops, clearly lost and distraught, Her navy hair bounces as she walks, yet she isn’t noticeable. She sits down to observe the crowded skyline only to have the quaint day ruined when a billboard for ‘Gift Developments’ catches her eye. This wasn’t acceptable, but she could correct it. She looked at her feet and a conveniently placed rock right next to her was practically begging her to throw it. She picked it up and immediately thought about throwing it at the billboard. The rock shredded through the paper, removing the face of a stock scientist with a massive hole.

 

She then sat down momentarily But it took only a second for her small moments of peace to be interrupted. There were tiny flying bits of rubble hurtling towards her. The ground rumbles below her as she feels disoriented and knows what kind of shake this is -the shake of a compromised building-; she quickly needs to find a crude shelter.

 

Running to investigate, she sees what seems to be some frivolous yet harmonious figures jump to the scene, regardless,  something happened suddenly like that, and there are people to save, it would be selfish just to sit there

 

Immediately, she sees someone partially under the rubble, they are lifting bits of wood and brick; she helps them out, and they immediately thank her and she makes sure the person isn't too worried or too scared. However, she can also sense many onlookers and their murmurs around her.

 

A rhythmic tap touched her ear, getting harder each second. That was human, She could hear how desperate they were to be saved, it wasn't their fault they were stuck under rubble but what could they do about it?

 

A bunch of women dressed somewhat in something uniform yet unique, helping people from the rubble, giving any of their aid to anyone who unfortunately got themselves mixed up, as one of the inwomen was searching, she heard her bag ping and give a little light, now was not the time, so she decided she would investigate the ping later.

 

The ragged girl could smell the gas from after the explosion, she couldn't smell anything nice, who would explode a building like that? Regardless, she continues to help people.

 

The ragged girl digs through the rubble until she gets a little startled by some faint pings, she sees what seems to be a woman in a ‘cute’ uniform and staff, with an instrument bag strapped to her back, with others either digging from other spots or comforting people.

“Help! I'm stuck, I want my mommy!”

 

“I'm going to help, just stay strong!” the ragged girl reassures 

 

“Help!”

 

Immediately, she managed to locate roughly where the girl might be, but there was a problem.

 

There was a chunk of brick too heavy to be kicked off by one person, but she still had to try some way, despite the wall not moving much, she would save that kid at any cost, despite how much work it would be to lift that chunk.

 

“Are you okay in there, can you stay strong for me?”

 

“I'll try, please get me out!”

 

Some people saw her effort and decided immediately to help her. With the added help, they got the child out easily. She was scared and disoriented but she wanted to be with her mother. She ran out of the rubble crying and squeezed her mother tight, clinging onto her like velcro, she seemed like she was grateful that she could live another day.

 

“Is that everyone from the complex accounted for?”

 

The neighbours looked at each other, and then at the girl. They were positive that everyone was accounted for.

 

She then investigates the rubble, what could have caused this? She then hurried back to her “home”, an abandoned building that she slightly uplifted the mood of by adding some decorations and doing some cleaning here and there, she boots up her ‘potato’ PC she got from scrapping parts she found in scrap yards.

 

Well, at least it runs old FPS games, certain silhouette doujin music videos, and much more, but mostly those things. At least she could play the games she wanted, they ran more than the processes above, which are over a century old at this point.

 

She played games on her computer, mindlessly sucked into it, she had no parents to care about her, so she was free to do as she pleased, it had been almost a year since she…escaped…the cake she saved money for was still in the fridge, she wanted to treat herself for her birthday today, it was a delightful strawberry cheesecake that looked like it was made in a bakery rather than manufactured, for that price, it better taste good.

 

As she finished playing games, she got the cheesecake out on a plate on the table. She wanted to be like a normal girl and celebrate her birthday like one. She placed a candle on the soft-firm cream on top of the cake, she got a match and attempted two times before a flame appeared, she lit the candle the way she saw on the tutorial the day before. She heard you can make a wish and blow it out if that works.

 

I wish…for a friend…

 

The candle goes out but as soon as the flame disappears, she hears a knock on some glass, she lifts her head to see a woman in a short dress, she looks for a clearer view of the woman she saw glimpses of, looks a little older than herself and had deeper skin with a slight yellow tone and braids arranged intricately with deep red ends, she had a red colour scheme and the little bag on her side was pulsating heavily.

“You talk… a lot…” the ragged girl muttered

 

“Oh okay, sometimes I do that.”

 

She was a little hesitant to let her in but what has she got to lose, besides, you only live once. She then opens the window and the woman jumps in.

“Oh, nice name, mine is…”

 

Did she have a name, her whole life she was referred to as “0271”, she couldn’t think of anything else, however, she was going to, to be honest, just make something from those numbers, it’s not like she cared what her name is anyways.

 

“Ah, rei…ni…” (Rei is 0, Ni is 2, she splits up the 4 numbers in her name to get Reini Nanaichi)

 

“Reini? Hmm rare, I mean so is mine.”

 

“Mhm”

 

“Well, this is going to be a long story, but uh, we fight against this evil force called the high council, they control everything and are hungry for power, we interfere with their affairs using the powers of the magical instruments, made by an expert instrument maker but more than half a millennium ago with the help of the higher being, known by so many other names, they also assigned this titan to the shrine just for like, more safekeeping?”

 

“Them…”

 

“Who?”

 

she wanted to be in.

 

Almost a year ago, she escaped from their lab. The ragged girl knew who was responsible for her suffering, she heard the high council tossed around in the lab more times than a Frisbie.

 

As soon as she heard their name mentioned, she knew they had to see some retribution. 



They made her.

 

They made her for selfish reasons. They kept her inside like a caged bird. They only wanted her for one reason.

 

Murder.

 

But she couldn't take up such an offer, something magical couldn't pick such a murderous husk of what people see as a human being.

 

She felt she wasn't worthy.

 

She felt it made a mistake, she was sure it made a mistake, and there is always a chance for an anomaly just like she is.

 

It began, one night, and she fell in agony. It was night, and there was a guard who just ignored every cry of pain she let out…

 

It hurt a lot.

 

Her stomach felt as if it was ripping itself. It was unlike any other pain; she was already on the floor crying and wincing as she was told to “get over it”, having no explanation for what it was.

 

She saw blood drip onto the floor in the dimly lit room. It was supposed to be bedtime, but she thought she was dying, it hurt too much, and she collapsed.

 

The door came off easily, and so did the guard’s head. It was all a blur, only remembering quick flashes and corpses slamming the floor. As soon as she could acknowledge the things around her, she was horrified as soon as she saw the amount of blood on her, it was all splattered onto her.

 

But she couldn't worry about that, right now she could hear sirens and planes, her feet felt a little pressed on, but she felt like something was telling her to make a run for it.

 

That voice felt like it was an aftermath, remnants of when she blacked out.

 

The corpse in front of her at that time made her sick, even now, being somewhat desensitised to gore; she would get slightly nauseous from the fact that she killed people.

 

But none of them are innocent…

 

They should rot in hell…

 

This isn't a nice memory.
