<h1> CHAPTER 5</H1>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Chapter 5;&nbsp;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It had been two weeks, and Kesshu saw that Reini was recovering, but she hadn&rsquo;t touched the magical keyboard yet, she was still too nervous to. But he could see her playing on the grand piano occasionally. One time as he walked through the hallway, he heard a beautiful symphony, it began off gently and then built up, and the motif was familiar too. He seemed flabbergasted because he knew how hard this song was. This random child they found was surely able to be something.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;La Campanella! Liszt!&rdquo; He said with excitement.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I guess everyone knows La Campanella. Maybe I should play something else after this. This is a beautiful piano.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;This is an ancient piano, all the way from the 19th century, I think the Meiji restoration, Mirai told me it belonged to a pianist, her name was Yuki, and her father started to learn how to make pianos after spending some time in Europe along with the typical traditional instruments the Otokabe family have been making instruments for many years, the instrument maker from long ago made this piano for his daughter and it&rsquo;s been in Tatsu&rsquo;s family since then, you can ask Mirai about it. It seems you have a knack for this stuff.&rdquo; Kesshu said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I think of playing the piano as a treat. If I behave well, I get music lessons. I also found some old MIDI keyboards in a random dumpster and practised.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I know you&rsquo;re resourceful, but try not to dumpster dive. That&rsquo;s not hygienic. I do understand it, though: no income, no parents.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Also do I have to go to school on the day after tomorrow?&rdquo; Reini asked, &ldquo;I&rsquo;m fine like this.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Yes, you have to go to school, there are many women who risked their lives for your right to go to school, only for you to say you don&rsquo;t want to go to school, you are going to school, and there will be no gift presence there because it&rsquo;s a nearby school, you&rsquo;ll like this school, if there are any problems you can tell me, we&rsquo;ll sort them out.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, what kind of school is it?&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;A school where the best educators in Japan are, the best money can buy, we think you'll bring out your best potential there, it&rsquo;s a full school, ranging from young children to high school 3rd years, you may have to introduce yourself when you go there.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I mean, I would have to.&rdquo;&nbsp;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I guess you could make some friends your age, hopefully, I wasn&rsquo;t sociable when I was at school, so I didn&rsquo;t make many friends.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I&rsquo;m weird, I doubt I&rsquo;ll make any friends.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;You&rsquo;ll always have me as a friend, I&rsquo;m always free!&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reini would remember that sentiment, she didn&rsquo;t know what friends were, but she wanted to know why so many people talked about having friends, Maybe it was like how close she was with Subject 6174; before she died by succumbing to her dystrophy. That&rsquo;s all she knew, she didn&rsquo;t even see her die because Gift thought it would be too much for her and distract her to witness her dying. Maybe they were right, being there as someone you knew died in front of you would certainly affect you, but at least she's got to see her. She remembered when she was first brought into the Tokyo lab from Greece. 6174 was super-intelligent but suffered from severe muscular dystrophy, so they brought her to the Tokyo lab to help develop a cure for her dystrophy.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">There were so many things she wanted to say to 6174, She didn&rsquo;t have a photo on her, to look at when she was down, only a memory, well, plenty of them, like when she first saw a bubble or the time when she stole a small slice of cake from the kitchen to give to the subject 6174; to see her smile as she ate that cake, that was certainly a moment Reini wanted to have forever.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Oh, 6174, only a constant to remember you by; how she changed everyone she met. And how she changed everyone upon her death and would have loved the world- if she was free.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mirai wandered the halls as she usually did, her stature was that of an average-sized woman and her face was that of an unageing titan that had seen people come and go while she stayed in her youthful cage of a body- was it a cage, at least she was given offerings, and praised, and many people asked her for help. She doesn&rsquo;t mind being here though, it was much better than space, much better than being around that disgusting individual Thanatos. Whatever, the past is the past and there are no time travellers here to help her, do time travellers exist? She only knew of Chronos but he quickly vanished after realising how bad of a person Thanatos was, many Titans vanished.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">But now the higher being assigned her here and she finally found peace. However she had a weird feeling lingering around her recently, it felt like you slightly remembered something you forgot about mid-conversation or how your whole family was there but you felt as if somebody was missing.&nbsp;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She knew the Otokabe family was now in small numbers, only picking up auras from 2 living family members, Tatsu didn&rsquo;t want to remarry and only the Higher Being knew who that second member was, if only she could investigate further. She knew Tatsu&rsquo;s daughter was killed off over twelve years ago, during World War 4.&nbsp;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mirai checked the supply cupboards, her pads went missing, and she was saving these for next month. But she wasn&rsquo;t mad. She knew someone probably needed them so she would bring up that point during dinner.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mirai also wanted to see Reini transform, but Reini was always hesitant- Mirai only wanted to see how her body reacted to the powers, just to see if she needed any adjustments. Before Reini started school, Mirai got Reini to train beforehand and was confident that Reini could transform. Nothing was stopping Reini, she could do it physically but Mirai wanted Reini to go ahead because she was still anxious.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She walked through the hallways and heard some piano playing, she slowly opened the divider to hear Claire de Lune, Mirai was impressed with her playing, and she slowly went up to Reini. Reini was too fixated on her playing to notice Miari behind her shoulder, she felt something behind her back and her music went to a sudden stop.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She slowly turned to see Mirai looking at her.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, hey Mirai, do you need something?&rdquo; Reini said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh yes, it&rsquo;s time for training,&rdquo; Mirai said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;But you said I was exempt today.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Well, just one little thing, pretty please!&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Fine then.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reini followed Mirai to the facility. Mirai pressed a remote and the lights turned off, with a spotlight being the only thing illuminating the room; and up came a small podium with a keyboard bag, Mirai knew what she was doing.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Take it,&rdquo; Mirai said, &ldquo;I think you&rsquo;ve earned it.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reini wasn&rsquo;t sure about it, she was hesitant to touch it even. She just stared at it awkwardly and Mirai let out a little sigh.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Young lady, if you don&rsquo;t take it, may the higher being be merciful!&rdquo; Mirai exclaimed.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She didn&rsquo;t know if she should stay quiet or apologise, she wasn&rsquo;t sure what to do, so she slowly approached the bag and unzipped it, she saw it was quite a unit; an Otokabe keyboard, after all, instruments were their speciality, and as well as a 6.25 it had a 3.5mm jack in the back for midi connections, she knew how much of a nuisance the bigger jack ports were, she felt it was better for midi keyboards just to have a 3.5mm port as it&rsquo;s easier to find 3.5mm wires.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mirai noticed Reini inspecting the unit; Mirai could tell she wanted to inspect the instrument rather than use it. Mirai didn&rsquo;t want to nudge the poor girl further.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;You press the transform button and play a certain quick melody to allow you to transform,&rdquo; Mirai said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The room was a little silent at the moment. Mirai talked again.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;It also picks up on your intent to transform.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Yet again, the room was silent.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I&rsquo;ll show you the melody,&rdquo; Mirai added.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She pulled out a sheet from a pocket and unfolded it- sheet music, titled &lsquo;transforming melody&rsquo;. It seemed to be at a measure of 4/4 and a bpm of 120, something usually typical, most of the chords were heavenly and it was only a quick 3-second flourish of major-based notes that mostly seemed uplifting.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, thanks.&rdquo; Reini said, &ldquo;This button, right?&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Yes&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She did as she was told and then after a bright flash, her entire outfit and hair changed; now drilled pigtails, fell and dangled a little, her stance conveyed a sense of perplexion, her knees met and her shoulders slanted.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Did I flash you, during the transformation sequence?&rdquo; Reini muttered as she held her skirt down.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Ah, no, well, you sort of flash-banged my eyes though. All I see, and everyone else, is a bright white light flash for a second and then your outfit changes,&rdquo; Mirai explained.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Huh, I always thought they had to wait for the transformation sequence, like let's say I confront a villain or something and then I transform.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Well it feels like that or something, for you, you go through a transformation sequence, but for us, you change in a second, the uniform helps you with certain attacks and adapts during certain environments.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Phew, I thought I would be attacked during the sequence.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Ah, I knew someone who was as surprised as you were, surprised that magical girls didn't go through the sequence as enemies are waiting.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;That&rsquo;s convenient.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mirai wheels over a mirror to Reini; so she can see what she had transformed into. It took her a second to truly grasp the change. Her hair is tied up in two neat coils rather than free wavy hair- her typical style.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In all honesty, she hadn&rsquo;t been this well dressed, not since she went out almost a month ago, she felt like some sort of princess, she felt pretty for once. She wanted to cry, tears of joy.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;So, I figured you like the outfit then, right?&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reini stood in silence in front of the mirror. She turned to Mirai.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Why yes,&rdquo; she replied.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Good, the outfit will always fit the age and style preference of the user, if you look under the skirt, there are yoga shorts rather than just pants </span><b>(or underwear for my American readers),</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> I&rsquo;ll be honest, yoga shorts are a lifesaver, I just don&rsquo;t feel comfortable with just pants, one, they expose too much for me and two, what if I leave a snail trail by accident or something!&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, you call it a snail trail?&rdquo; Reini said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Well, I mean it makes sense. Doesn&rsquo;t it?&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh yeah.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">They stood there in silence, the only noise being white noise hissing from the ventilators. They blinked at each other, not sure what topic to bring up.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Ahem, have you seen my pads anywhere, I have such a bad sense of direction,&rdquo; Mirai asked.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I took them, I kinda used up a lot.&rdquo; Reini said.&rdquo; I&rsquo;m sorry, they were so heavy, I did put them away in the bathroom drawers when I was done, there is no point really in putting them back I guess, there&rsquo;s only one pad left, used them a lot last week, I will pay you back if you want.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh no, poor thing, you don&rsquo;t have to pay for hygiene products in this house, you don&rsquo;t have to pay for anything except for luxuries you want with your allowance, you should get heavy flow pads&rdquo; Mirai said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;You mean that?&rdquo; Reini asked in confusion.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;You are a minor under our responsibility and you are also a talent, by being a talent you perform and you get compensated, thus technically giving you an allowance, we have to get your idol uniform tailored next week, can&rsquo;t wear the same thing when you perform and fight evil.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Ah, alright then, will it be adorable,&rdquo; Reini asked.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Yes, I&rsquo;ll make sure of it too, by the way, I forgot to tell you it&rsquo;s such short notice too but one of our acts from Europe is coming over for the weekend, I'm sorry I didn&rsquo;t inform you sooner.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;It&rsquo;s fine, I understand.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, I&rsquo;m very glad then, she&rsquo;s from Ukraine </span><b>(this is for that Ukrainian girl from my school who&rsquo;s probably done with year 13, she asked for this)</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and this is also some very important SII stuff, I&rsquo;ll check if Tatsu is okay with you sitting in on in the meeting.&rdquo;&nbsp;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Why might I have to sit in the meeting?&rdquo; Reini asked.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;It might concern you,&rdquo; Mirai said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Alright, I got that, uh how do you de-transform?&rdquo; Reini asked.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Ah, got that, is it okay if I take a picture, it&rsquo;s your first time and I thought I&rsquo;d keep a memory of that, and you would just play the melody in minor to de-transform, yet again it picks up your intent so you don&rsquo;t do it on accident.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Go ahead, I appreciate you asking.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She took out her camera and a picture printed, the pose was obvious but it looked a little stiff. She had a neutral expression and her hands joined in the middle. It was certainly a modest pose compared to her frivolous outfit.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mirai shows Reini the photo briefly, she then looks at it and then gives it back, she plays the melody in minor and then de-transforms, she&rsquo;s back to normal again.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Well, how was that?&rdquo; She asks.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I felt different&hellip; for sure,&rdquo; Reini replied.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She was most definitely different, for sure. She wasn&rsquo;t sure what it was but she was most definitely different in some way. She went back up with Mirai and saw someone at the door, she had warm deep brown hair and freckles and looked tall and attractive, she wore an office-style suit with a bow scarf and had rectangular glasses, she also had a briefcase with her.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Is that her?&rdquo; she quietly asked Kesshu.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Yes.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She could hear them, regardless of how quiet they were, the hallways were empty of sound. The woman slowly approached Reini, not with a slow cautious pace but an easier pace of walking.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Hello, I'm Vera, a part-time popstar and licensed neurologist/ therapist, what&rsquo;s your name?.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Good morning,&rdquo; Reini replied bluntly.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh uh, good morning then,&rdquo; Vera said</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reini stared at her for a moment, and then she blinked and jumped in realisation, she just said her name was &lsquo;good morning&rsquo; Who did that, was she sort of messed up after the transformation?</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;No sorry, it&rsquo;s Reini, I am sorry,&rdquo; Reini added.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Well, it&rsquo;s nice to meet you Reini, I'm meeting with Mr. Otokabe and I&rsquo;ll also have a performance in Tokyo.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh okay, well, I&rsquo;ll be in my room if you need me,&rdquo; Reini said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She went to her room bored out of her mind but noticed something different, was it the lighting? She walked around her room mindlessly. And then she heard a knock on her door.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Reini?&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She opened the door to see Mirai with many things, an assortment of old and new things. Mirai came into the room and put the stuff on the bed. Reini watched as she sorted the things out slightly.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I have things for you,&rdquo; Mirai said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Really?&rdquo; Reini said, &ldquo;I have far too many things!&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Far too many? Your drawers are empty, and you have a figure, just a figure, some of these are old things, hand-me-downs, but I did get you some new things.&rdquo; Mirai said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I think this is enough, Mirai,&rdquo; Reini said</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;What do you mean this is all enough!&rdquo; Mirai exclaimed, &ldquo;I am simply filling up your drawers.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I think it&rsquo;s fine, thanks a lot.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">There was too much stuff for Reini to handle, she didn&rsquo;t know what to do with it, it was too much for her to handle, what did she even do to deserve all this? It felt like a trick, she wasn&rsquo;t used to all this affection. Perhaps it was because she was never given this many things, let alone a room like she is entitled to. It all felt weird for her, this didn&rsquo;t feel normal for her.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, Vera wants to see you by the way!&rdquo; Mirai said, &ldquo;I just got it through my phone, she should be in the living room!&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Oh, okay.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">She went to the living room, a little scared because she knew that being called in by someone meant she was in trouble, that&rsquo;s how she did it when she was captive, she hadn&rsquo;t been in any sort of captivity or school for a year, she mostly took online classes to keep her mind stimulated.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Hello Reini, sit anywhere you want, I mean, you live here?&rdquo; Vera said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Am I in&hellip; oh sorry, old habit,&rdquo; Reini said.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;No, it&rsquo;s okay, think of my face as a face you can trust, I know we just met today so let&rsquo;s take it easy.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Got it.&rdquo;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reini was non-confrontational, but she had so much she wanted to tell her, but she didn&rsquo;t want to be judged. Would she think she would be too weird if she went directly with her issues?</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;Well, let's start, how was this week?&rdquo; Vera asked</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;I'm not sure, I felt a little overwhelmed with everything, but I guess I'm settling in here great, but I don't understand why they're being so nice to me,&rdquo; Reini said&nbsp;</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">&ldquo;So you feel overwhelmed?&rdquo; Vera replied.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
